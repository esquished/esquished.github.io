ABOUT

Squished is a band without a plan—No plan whatsover—We come together, play, and then leave. We might record an artifact of these playings, other times we might not. If we like what it sounds like, it normally ends up on Bandcamp.

Someday, Squished might actually have a set of songs we can play for people other than ourselves.
